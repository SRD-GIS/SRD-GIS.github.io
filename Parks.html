Parks

The Strathcona Regional District provides park service in 3 of the 4 electoral areas.
Regional parkland constitutes 667 hectares / 1,648 acres throughout the Discovery Islands and Area D (South of Campbell River).

Regional Parks & Public Beach Access

In 2015, the regional district undertook an analysis of all the unopened road right of ways that terminated at waters edge. This project took place on Quadra and Cortes Islands as well as in Area D (Jubilee Prkwy South to the Oyster River).
Each site was assessed and ranked into a three phased development. The sites deemed most suitable for immediate signage were all sign-posted with uniformed signage:



Look for these signs to access all the beautiful beaches throughout the regional district.
