Parks

Explore pebble and shell beaches to a backdrop of expansive blue seas and majestic mountain ranges. Breathe the fresh forest air and soothe the soul as you hike along remote trails through moss laden rainforests. The Strathcona Regional District is blessed with a network of beautiful parks, trails and beaches dotting the Discovery Islands, Cortes Island and Area D (South of Campbell River).
